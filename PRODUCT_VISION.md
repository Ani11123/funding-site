# PRODUCT VISION

The website should feel like:

"Your trusted funding advisor"

Not:

"Another finance company"

Desired Brand Personality:

* Trustworthy
* Premium
* Professional
* Fast
* Helpful
* Human

Visual Direction:

* Premium finance design
* Modern SaaS feel
* High trust
* Clean layouts
* Mobile-first

Colour Palette:

Primary:
#081F3F (Deep Navy)

Secondary:
#D4AF37 (Champagne Gold)

Accent:
#0F766E (Teal)

Neutral:
#F8FAFC

Text:
#0F172A

Avoid:

* Bright red
* Cheap gradients
* Crypto styling
* Overly dark layouts
