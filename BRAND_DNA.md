# BRAND DNA

## Brand Name
Nexora Funding Limited

## Brand Tagline
Funding Growth. Building Futures.

## Brand Personality

Honest — We say what we mean. No jargon, no small print surprises.
Intelligent — We understand business finance deeply and guide with expertise.
Professional — Every interaction reflects quality and reliability.
Innovative — We find funding solutions others cannot.
Calm — We remove the stress from a complex process.
Helpful — Our measure of success is your outcome.

## Brand Voice

DO use:
- Clear, confident language
- Plain English (no banking jargon)
- Advisor-led tone ("our advisors", "our team")
- UK spellings

DO NOT use:
- Salesy superlatives ("UK's Leading", "Best In Class")
- Fake urgency ("Act Now!", "Limited Time")
- Unverified statistics
- FCA regulatory claims unless confirmed

## Official Colour Palette (Nexora Brand Kit)

Primary Navy:    #0B2E59  — Dominant brand colour (headers, dark sections, text)
Orange:          #C76A1B  — Primary accent (buttons, links, icons, CTA highlights, hover states)
Dark Navy:       #082347  — Dark sections background (TopBar, Footer)
Light Grey:      #F4F5F7  — Page background (light sections)
Charcoal:        #333333  — Body text

Supporting:
  Secondary Blue:  #4F6D8A  — Supporting UI elements
  Orange hover:    #a85918  — Button/link hover state

Extended palette (available, use sparingly):
  Warm Sand:    #F5E6D2
  Steel Blue:   #B6C4D6
  Sage Green:   #A7BFA6
  Gold:         #D4A037

### Usage rules
- Navy: dominant background, headings, structural elements
- Orange: ALL interactive elements — buttons, link hovers, icon highlights, active states, CTA accents
- Never use the old bronze/gold (#B08D57) — fully replaced by orange #C76A1B

## Typography

Headings:  Montserrat (600/700/800/900) — primary heading font
Body:      Inter (300–900) — all body copy, UI labels, captions
Fallback:  system-ui, sans-serif

Heading style:
- Extrabold or Bold weight
- Tight line-height (1.08–1.15)
- Proper tracking (tight on large headlines)
- Applied via globals.css h1/h2/h3/h4 rule + inline font-family for SVG text

## Logo

The Nexora logo mark is a geometric N letterform:
- Left stroke: thick vertical bar (navy #0B2E59)
- Diagonal stroke: left-to-right diagonal (navy #0B2E59)
- Right side: three ascending orange (#C76A1B) bar-chart columns (representing growth)
- Arrow: small right-triangle at the top-right corner (orange #C76A1B, growth direction)

  public/assets/logo/nexora-mark.svg  — standalone N mark
  public/assets/logo/logo.svg         — full wordmark (light backgrounds)

## Visual Language

- Clean, generous whitespace
- Premium card components with subtle borders
- Orange accent used confidently for CTAs and interactive elements
- Dark navy gradient backgrounds in hero and dark sections
- Subtle diagonal stripe texture in hero (low opacity)
- Smooth, purposeful animations (22s hero zoom, hover lifts, marquee)

## What We Are NOT

- Not a bank
- Not a lender
- Not a fintech startup
- Not a marketplace

We are: Independent business funding advisors.

## Content Rules (Hard)

- No AI features, no customer portal, no calculator, no lender marketplace
- No unverified statistics (no "500+ businesses funded", "£150M arranged" type claims)
- No FCA regulated claims unless reference number confirmed
- No fake testimonials — all marked TODO until real ones provided
- No copyrighted or competitor imagery
- No real lender logos without explicit permission
- Original copywriting only
- Phase 1 is lead generation only
